Handmade ceramics brand Ker is based in Reykjavik, Iceland All items by Ker ardesigned and handmade on the wheel by artist Guðbjörg Káradóttir Guðbjörg works with stoneware and porcelain in her work. She developed a method in mixing Volcanic ash from Southern Peninsula region in Iceland in porcelain. Regarding her inspiration, Icelandic nature has always played a big part in Guðbjorg’s work
